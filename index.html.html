What I Found

West Gear is crushing it
They're making $243M compared to Walmart's $75M. That's a huge difference and made me wonder what they're doing differently.
West Coast loves Adidas
30% of all sales come from the West region. Makes sense given the active lifestyle culture there.
Men's street shoes are the winner
$209M in revenue - way more than any other category. Street fashion is definitely big business.
Walmart underperforming
Despite being a major retailer, Walmart only did $75M. There might be pricing or positioning issues here.

Ideas for Improvement

Focus more on the West: Since it's already the strongest region, putting more inventory and marketing there could boost sales even more.
Figure out West Gear's secret: They're doing something right. Maybe better store locations, customer service, or product selection?
Help women's athletic category: It's the lowest performer at $107M. Could use better marketing or different product designs.
Work with Walmart: There's definitely room to improve this partnership. Maybe different product mix or pricing strategy.
Boost online sales: In-store still dominates, but online has huge potential especially after COVID changed shopping habits.
